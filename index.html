What is Presence

More Than a
Conference.

Presence is SIBKL's annual gathering for the next generation — a convergence of youth, young adults, and students from across Malaysia and beyond.

This isn't a hype event. It's a holy collision — where heaven meets earth, where the ordinary becomes extraordinary, and where the generation of this moment finds its voice.

"Where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them."
— Matthew 18:20 · The Anchor Verse
